About Our School

Shaping the Leaders of Tomorrow

Maharishi Vidya Mandir Senior Secondary School is a premier institution committed to providing holistic education. We combine academic rigour with moral values, creative thinking, and physical development to produce well-rounded individuals.

Our dedicated faculty, modern infrastructure, and student-first philosophy create an environment where every child thrives. We offer a broad curriculum spanning languages, sciences, mathematics, and commerce — preparing students for higher education and life beyond the classroom.

Guided by the teachings of Maharishi, we instil discipline, respect, and a love for learning that stays with students throughout their lives.
